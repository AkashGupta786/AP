    Official App Store Logos and Badges

    Apple provides App Store badges for iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple TV that can be used in marketing materials. These badges are available in 40 localizations and come in black (preferred) and white (alternative) versions. Apple’s guidelines specify that the App Store service mark always appears in English, and the gray border around the black badge should not be modified. You can download badges, app icons, or QR codes and generate embeddable code linking directly to your app’s store page.
